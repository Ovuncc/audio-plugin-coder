# Creative Brief: Osmium

## Vision
**Osmium** is the ultimate "make it punch" button. A single, futuristic knob that instantly adds weight, impact, and high-density saturation to any sound source. It combines the clean snap of a transient shaper, the glue of a limiter, and the harmonic richness of a warm tube drive into one seamless motion.

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Theme:** Futuristic, Minimal, Industrial.
- **Key Element:** One massive, glowing central rotatable control (The Core) that changes color/intensity as it is turned up.
- **Palette:** Dark brushed metal, neon accents (Cyan/Orange), high contrast.

## Core Behavior
The "Osmium" knob is a macro that controls these internal processors in parallel:
1.  **Transient Shaper:** Boosts the attack phase to cut through the mix.
2.  **Tube Saturation:** Introduces even-order harmonics for warmth and perceived loudness.
3.  **Limiter:** transparently catches peaks to prevent clipping while allowing aggressive drive.

## Target Audience
- Electronic music producers needing quick impact.
- Sound designers looking for "instant banger" processing.
- Mix engineers wanting a secret sauce for drums and bass.
